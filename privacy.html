01 Scope & Application

This Privacy Policy applies to Farm Tractor Simulator 2025 ("the Game"), developed and published by Mob Tech Gamerz ("we", "us", or "our"). It governs how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you download, install, or interact with the Game on any supported platform including iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.

By using the Game, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described herein, please discontinue use of the Game immediately.

02 Data We Collect

We aim to minimize data collection. The following table outlines the categories of information that may be gathered during your use of the Game:

Data Category Examples Linked to You?
Usage Data Session duration, feature interactions, levels played No
Identifiers Advertising identifier (IDFA), vendor identifier No
Device Info Device model, OS version, screen resolution No
Crash Logs Error reports, stack traces, performance data No
We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos, or any other personally identifiable information directly from our users.

03 Tracking Technologies

The Game may use the following technologies to gather non-personal data:

  • Advertising SDKs — Third-party ad networks may use advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness.
  • Analytics Tools — We use anonymized analytics to understand gameplay patterns, identify bugs, and improve overall game quality.
  • Local Storage — Game progress, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

On iOS 14.5 and later, advertising tracking requires your explicit consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may decline tracking at any time.

04 How We Use Your Data

Any data collected is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Delivering and maintaining core game functionality
  • Displaying advertisements to support the free-to-play model
  • Analyzing aggregated usage trends to improve game content and performance
  • Diagnosing technical issues and resolving crash reports
  • Ensuring compliance with applicable laws and platform policies

05 Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, non-personal data may be shared with trusted third-party partners in the following scenarios:

  • Ad Networks — To serve and optimize in-game advertisements (e.g., Google AdMob, Unity Ads).
  • Analytics Providers — To process aggregated, de-identified performance and usage data.
  • Legal Obligations — When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

All third-party partners are contractually obligated to handle data in compliance with applicable privacy regulations.

06 Data Retention

Anonymized analytics data is retained for a maximum of 24 months to identify long-term gameplay trends. Crash logs and diagnostic data are automatically purged after 90 days. Locally stored game progress remains on your device until you uninstall the Game or manually clear its data.

07 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect the information associated with the Game, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Restricted access to analytics dashboards with role-based permissions
  • Regular security audits of third-party SDK integrations
  • Compliance with Apple's App Store data handling guidelines

While no system is completely impervious to risk, we are committed to maintaining reasonable and appropriate protective measures.

08 Children's Privacy

Farm Tractor Simulator 2025 is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). If you believe we have inadvertently gathered information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will take prompt steps to delete it.

09 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may be entitled to exercise certain rights regarding your data:

  • Opt-Out of Tracking — Use your device's Settings → Privacy → Tracking to disable ad tracking for this app.
  • Access & Deletion — Request details about any data we hold or ask for its deletion by contacting us.
  • Reset Advertising ID — You can reset your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings.
  • Withdraw Consent — Where data processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

We will respond to valid requests within 30 calendar days.

Continued use of the Game after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
