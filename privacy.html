Privacy Policy

Helicopter Flight Pilot 3D

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how Helicopter Flight Pilot 3D ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you interact with our mobile application. We value your trust and are dedicated to maintaining transparency about our data practices.

Section 01

Overview of Data Collection

Helicopter Flight Pilot 3D may collect certain categories of data to enhance your gameplay experience, improve app performance, and deliver relevant content. The data we handle falls into the following categories:

Usage Data

Information about how you interact with the App, such as session duration, features accessed, and in-game actions performed.

Identifiers

Device-level identifiers used for analytics and advertising attribution purposes, including advertising identifiers provided by the operating system.

Section 02

How We Use Your Data

The information collected through the App is utilized for the following purposes:

  • Improving overall app stability, performance, and user experience
  • Understanding gameplay patterns to refine missions, controls, and difficulty balancing
  • Delivering relevant advertisements within the App through third-party ad networks
  • Tracking the effectiveness of advertising campaigns and measuring user acquisition metrics
  • Diagnosing technical issues, crashes, and bugs to ensure reliable performance
Section 03

Tracking and Advertising

Our App may use identifiers and usage data to track your activity across apps and websites owned by third parties. This tracking enables us and our advertising partners to deliver personalized advertisements and measure their effectiveness.

You can limit ad tracking through your device settings by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or adjusting your advertising preferences in the system privacy settings.

We work with third-party advertising partners who may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies for a comprehensive understanding of their data practices.

Section 04

Third-Party Services

Helicopter Flight Pilot 3D integrates with third-party services for analytics, advertising, and distribution. These services operate under their own privacy policies and may collect data independently. Key third-party integrations include:

  • Advertising networks for displaying in-app advertisements
  • Analytics platforms for monitoring app performance and user engagement
  • Apple App Store for app distribution and purchase processing

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies for detailed information.

Section 05

Data Retention and Storage

Usage data and identifiers collected through the App are retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes.

Game progress, helicopter selections, and mission completion data are stored locally on your device. This local data is not transmitted to external servers and can be removed by uninstalling the application or clearing app data through your device settings.

Section 06

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate steps to remove such data.
